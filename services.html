What Are They?

A lot goes into making a website functional, visually appealing, lead-generating, money-making, and accesible. We will go through your website and check for a variety of issues, errors, and ways to improve.

What Is Covered In An Audit?

During a basic audit, we'll go through your entire website and give you feedback on your website:

  • functionality (we will test for broken links/images, bugs, technical issues, etc)
  • layout & user experience
  • design (& best practices to implement)

If you opt for a full audit, we will also include feedback on your website's:

  • accessibility (this is SUPER important!)
  • SEO
  • Optimization (website speed and loading issues)

Is An Audit Right For Me?

A website audit is probably your best bet if:

  • You know that something (or more!) needs to change on your website, but you're not sure what they are.
  • You're not looking to work through any issues, you just want a checklist of things you need to change/upgrade.
  • You'd rather do the work on your own instead of hiring someone, but you also want to make sure you are doing the right work.

It might NOT be a fit if:

  • You are just starting out and need more of a strategy.
  • You'd prefer to talk through your website questions with someone, or have someone do the work for you.
  • You plan on burning your site down and starting over soon.
