This client needed a website to gain more clients for his welding business. He was starting from scratch and needed web design, development, build, and hosting.

Overview

The client was working through a freelancing website to get clients and wanted a way to take his business up a notch. He had many reviews and images, but no way to be found through a web search beyond the freelancing platform.
He needed a website (including design and development) and hosting. The biggest need was to have a way for potential clients to reach him.

Approach

I designed and built a sleek website that showed off Alex's services, his past work, and client testimonials. His instagram feed was integrated as a gallery page and a contact page listing his service area was added.
His site is hosted on our server. This way, I can monitor the site and jump in if anything goes wrong, rather than him trying to troubleshoot on his own.
We also set his site up to monitor user interaction to improve his site in the future.

Results

Within two days of launch, a new client reached out to Alex through his website and the job was completed that weekend. The SEO we added to his site is helping customers find him through search engines.
