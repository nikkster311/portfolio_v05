Dichotomous Key (Pacific Northwest Plants)

React application

Figure out what plant you're looking at based on its characteristics.

Codebase: React, CSS, and responsive design.

This was one of my very first projects. I had always seen dichotomous keys in books, telling you to turn to a certain page based on if a plant had this or that. At the end it would tell you what plant you were looking at. I wanted to create an online version, however I could not find a database or API that I could pull from, and didn't know how to create my own yet.

Created in March 2019, Version 01 launched March 2019
