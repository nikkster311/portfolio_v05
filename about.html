Meet Nikki

Nikki is the driving force behind Nikkster.tech. They have been coding professionally since 2020 and unprofessionally since the Myspace days. Since then, they have built and edited sites for multiple E-commerce brands, small businesses, and organizations.

At Nikkster.tech, Nikki takes all the knowledge they've gained from designers, developers, and product managers over the years and brings them directly to you. They combines their passion of technology and helping others by providing clients with modern, sleek websites that not only help their clients reach their goals, but also exceed their expectations.

Originally from Southern California, Nikki spent 5 years working in National Parks across the USA. Before that, they worked with local non-profits on active transportation and public land initiatives and taught ecology and water education in the local schools. Currently they reside in Washington State, where they enjoy the easy access to trees and water.

When they decided to take the leap and make a career switch, Nikki noticed that empathy & communication skills were lacking in the tech world. That's why they strive to:

  • design beautiful and functional sites that acheive their clients' goals
  • create sites that work flawlessly on both desktop and mobile (we live in a mobile age, after all!)
  • be upfront & honest about expectations, milestones, and cost
  • try to reach clients within their budget
  • keep clients in the loop, always
  • build exceptional websites that their clients will love
